Advanced

Once you learn basic combos with your character, you can learn more advanced combos. You can learn how to do advanced things such as FADC (Focus attack dash cancel).

FADC

This is used to either cancel moves or to utilize the focus attack's super armor to take a hit without losing health. This is an example of an FADC combo.

cr.lp/cr.mp > cl.hp xx Hadouken xx FADC > cr.hp xx MK Ryusokyaku > cr.lp/cr.mp xx LK Tatsu > HP Shoryuken

The slashes mean you can do either move. cr.lp/cr.mp means you can either use crouching light punch or crouching medium punch. Most of the time it is based on preference and which move you think is best.
