About

Counter Strike: Global Offensive is one of the fastest-pased, reflex based games in the market right now. With over 20 million copies purchased, its 4th in most owned game and 2nd in most players on Steam. It is available for Windows, Mac, Linux, and even Xbox 360 and PS3.

The Counter Strike series is one of the oldest but still popular first person shooter games. The first Counter Strike game came out in 1999. CS:GO is the 4th installment in the series coming out in 2012.

Game Modes

It has 5 different game modes. To learn more about how to play each game mode, visit the How to play page.

  • Classic Casual and Classic Competitive:
    • Bomb Defusal
    • Hostage Rescue
  • Demolition
  • Arms Race
  • Deathmatch (PC Only)
